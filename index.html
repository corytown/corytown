Cory the Corgi wants to try the new mango bubblegum chocolate-chip swirl ice cream at Cathy the Cat’s Ice Cream Shop. Join Cory on an adventure as he embarks on a journey from his house to obtain a truly sweet prize at the end! In order to arrive safely at the shop, you’ll have to help him navigate the streets of his neighborhood, interact with the people he encounters on the way, and make smart and safe decisions.

How to Play

  • Goal of the game: Help Cory make wise choices about peer pressure, meeting strangers, and navigating the streets of his neighborhood. Use the mouse or the enter key to navigate each situation.
  • If you help Cory make the right decision, he'll get closer to the ice cream shop. Be on the lookout for the explanations behind the right answers and make sure you stay on the safe path!
  • If you and Cory make the wrong choice, there will be consequences and Cory will get farther away from the ice cream shop. Keep an eye out for tips to help you make the correct choices in the future!

Corytown Was Made...

  • At the Pixar Girls Who Code 2017 Summer Immersion Program using Unity, Fungus, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, QuickTime Player, and Final Cut Pro. It was created within the span of a week and a half as the group's final project for the program.
  • With the purpose of teaching kids how to be aware of their surroundings and how to develop the street smarts necessary in today's world to be safe in any kind of environment. These skills can be applied anywhere from a small-town neighborhood to the center of a bustling city.
  • By Alison Tso, Charisa Shin, Sabrina Stone, and Terryn Jung -- four girls passionate about computer science and making a difference in their communities.
